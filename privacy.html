Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

Overview

Ranita AI respects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from your device. We do not track your activity, access your contacts, or collect identifiers.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, IP address)
  • Usage analytics or crash logs
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files

How Ranita AI Works

Ranita AI is designed to operate without transmitting user data. Any preferences you set are stored locally on your device and never leave your phone.

Third-Party Services

Ranita AI does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect the changes.

Children’s Privacy

Ranita AI does not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and post the revised policy on this page.
