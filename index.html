Why We Built Ranita AI

It started with a simple text message — one that looked completely legitimate. Within days, the scammers had convinced them to transfer money to "protect" their account from fraud. By the time they realized what had happened, the money was gone. But what hurt even more than the financial loss was watching the shame, the embarrassment, and the self-doubt that followed. "How could I have been so foolish?" they kept asking.

The truth is, they weren't foolish. They were trusting. And scammers exploit that trust every single day.

The numbers are staggering. Billions of dollars are stolen from people every year through fraudulent messages, fake calls, and deceptive schemes. But what the statistics don't capture is the human cost — the retirees who lose their nest eggs, the grandparents who drain their savings trying to help a "grandchild in trouble," the hardworking people who see decades of careful saving vanish in an instant.

These aren't just financial crimes. They're life-altering events. I've seen victims fall into depression. Marriages strained by guilt and blame. People who lose their sense of independence because they no longer trust their own judgment.

It doesn't have to be this way.

That's why we built Ranita AI. We wanted to create something simple — a tool that anyone can use to check if a message or conversation is suspicious before it's too late. No complicated setup. No confusing technology. Just paste a message or take a screenshot, and get a clear, honest answer.

We also made a promise to ourselves: your privacy would never be compromised. Every analysis happens right on your phone using Apple Intelligence. Your messages are never sent to any server. They're never stored in any database. They're never seen by anyone but you. Because the last thing someone worried about a scam needs is another reason not to trust technology.
